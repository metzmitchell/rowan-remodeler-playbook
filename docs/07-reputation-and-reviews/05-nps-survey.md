# Net Promoter Survey (NPS)

For membership and recurring transaction businesses, use a tool like [**GatherUp.com**](http://gatherup.com/) and connect to **Google My Business** for 10/10 reviews. If they leave a 9/10 or lower, they can see a message that asks to give feedback.
