# NPS Survey Guide

Guidance on creating and configuring Net Promoter Score surveys for client feedback collection.

## Setup

For membership and recurring transaction businesses, use a tool like [**GatherUp.com**](http://gatherup.com/) and connect to **Google My Business** for automated review routing.

## Configuration

- **10/10 reviews**: Automatically route to Google My Business for public posting
- **9/10 or lower**: Show feedback request message to gather detailed feedback before it becomes a public review
