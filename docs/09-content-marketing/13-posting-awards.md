# Posting Awards SOP
### **Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for Posting Awards on WordPress Blog**

---

### **1. Preparation**

- **Gather Information:** Collect all necessary details about the award including the award logo, the press release, and any text that needs to be included in the blog post.
- **Stock Photography:** Download or select a suitable generic stock image for the blog post’s featured image.
    
    Refer image size [here]([Link to your Design Guidelines]) [Deliverables List Source - [Your Name]]([Link to your Spreadsheet])
    

---

### **2. Update Portfolio Page**

- **Access WordPress Dashboard:**
    - Login to your WordPress site.
    - Go to the ‘Pages’ section.
- **Edit Portfolio Page:**
    - Find the portfolio page that you need to update.
    - Add a new section or update an existing one with details about the award.
    - Ensure to mention the ‘Home’ indicated in the award.
    - Insert the award logo and a brief description.

---

### **3. Update Awards Page**

- **Access Awards Page:**
    - Go to the ‘Pages’ section in the WordPress dashboard.
    - Locate and edit the Awards page.
- **Add New Entry:**
    - Insert the award details and hyperlink this entry to the corresponding blog post.

---

### **4. Update Awards Spreadsheet**

- **Open Spreadsheet:**
    - Locate the awards spreadsheet on your preferred platform [awards spreadsheet]([Link to your Spreadsheet]).
- **Add New Entry:**
    - Enter the award details including the
        - Organization
        - Name of the Award
        - Year(s)
        - Homes
        - Company received
        - Order (if necessary)

---

### **5. Create Blog Post**

- **Access WordPress Blog Section:**
    - Go to the ‘Posts’ section in the WordPress dashboard.
- **Add New Post:**
    - **Title:** Include the name of the award in the blog post title.
    - **Content:** Write a short and concise post about the award, pulling information from the provided press release. Ensure it’s engaging and fits the tone of your blog:
        - Mention why the award is important.
        - Include key details about the award.
        - Acknowledge any notable achievements or milestones that led to receiving the award.
        - Add the award logo and a brief statement from the press release at the end.
- **Featured Image:**
    - Set the previously selected generic stock photo as the featured image.
        
        Refer image size [here]([Link to your Design Guidelines]) [Deliverables List Source - [Your Name]]([Link to your Spreadsheet])
        

---

### **6. Publish and Promote**

- **Proofread:**
    - Carefully review the blog post for accuracy and typos.
- **Publish Post:**
    - Once satisfied, click ‘Publish’.
- **Share:**
    - Promote the blog post on your social media channels and any relevant platforms to increase visibility. [Link to Social Media SOP]

---

### **7. Confirmation**

- **Double-check:**
    - Verify that the portfolio page, awards page, and blog post are correctly updated and linked.
    - Ensure the awards spreadsheet reflects the new entry accurately.

---

### **Example Blog Post Structure:**

### **Title: [Award Name] - Honored to Receive this Prestigious Recognition!**

### **Content:**

We are thrilled to announce that [Your Organization] has been awarded the [Award Name]. This prestigious award recognizes our commitment to excellence and innovation in the [relevant industry/field].

[Brief details about the award and its significance.]

This achievement is a testament to the hard work and dedication of our team. We look forward to continuing our mission to [company mission or goal].

![Award Logo]

For more details, read the official press release here.

---

By following this SOP, you can ensure that each award is properly recognized and promoted on your WordPress blog, maintaining consistency and efficiency in your communications.
