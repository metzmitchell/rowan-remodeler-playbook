# Social Media Strategy

**Create a social media strategy for all platforms**

**Include:**

1. Goals
2. Which platforms to choose
3. Frequency
4. Target audience
5. Tone of voice per platform
6. Content mix
7. Examples to follow

## Checklist

- [ ]  Do social media takeovers on Instagram with local businesses
- [ ]  Create a social media strategy for all platforms
- [ ]  Content is high quality and visually consistent
- [ ]  Content authentically showcases what it's like to be a client
- [ ]  Content uses each platform natively and fully
- [ ]  Participate in local Facebook communities/promote your offerings
