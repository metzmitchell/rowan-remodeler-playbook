# Blog Content Strategy

**Create a blog content strategy**

**Includes:**

1. Goals
2. Categories
3. Frequency
4. Promotion
5. Implementation (who does it and budget required)

**IDEAS**

- Bring in subject matter experts to write content
- Have your clients "write" aka influence/inform your content so they re-share

## Checklist

- [ ]  Create a blog content strategy
- [ ]  Publish on a regular frequency
