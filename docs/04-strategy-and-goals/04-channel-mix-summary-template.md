# Channel Mix Summary Template

| Overarching Goal(s) | Leading Indicator(s) | Lagging Indicator(s) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **New Customer Revenue** | First-Time Purchases | New Customer Revenue (Monthly vs. Annual) |
| **Repeat Customer Revenue** | Number of Campaigns, Number of Flows/Automations | Repeat Purchase Rate |

| Channel | Details & Objectives | Primary KPI & Target | Secondary KPIs |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Website** | Performance and conversion optimization. | Conversion Rate (e.g., 7%) | Monthly Unique Visitors |
| **Blog Content** | Thought leadership and SEO value. | Content Velocity | Engagement Rate |
| **Email Marketing** | Retention and nurture sequences. | Open/Click Rate | List Growth Rate |
| **Social Media** | Awareness and engagement. | Engagement Rate | Reach/Impressions |
| **Advertising** | Direct response and traffic. | Cost Per Lead (CPL) | ROAS |
| **SEO** | Long-term organic visibility. | Organic Traffic | Keyword Rankings |
| **Offline/Traditional** | Print, events, and networking. | Response Rate | Brand Recall |
| **PR & Messaging** | Media coverage and brand voice. | Media Mentions | Share of Voice |
| **Referrals** | Strategic partnerships and clients. | Referral Rate | Partner LTV |
| **Directories** | Houzz, Google Business, etc. | View-to-Lead Rate | Review Rating |
