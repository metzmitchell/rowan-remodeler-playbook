# Ask these questions during the sales process

Status: Sales Strategies

Consider adding these fields to your CRM as well for better client/prospect data storage.

1. Where they heard about you
2. Size of business
3. Why are they looking for office now
4. Where did they come from before
5. Details about their business and what they need to perform
6. What their number one priority is in a space
7. Growth goals for the future
