# Customer Persona Questions

  

Understanding the profile and demographics of your buyers is essential for creating effective sales copy, ad creatives, content, paid ad settings, and so much more. Making sure all ads, creatives and content speak to the right audience makes a world of difference on improving conversions.

  

Buyers List: A buyers list is a spreadsheet of your customers that helps gain deeper insights into their demographic information when imported into tools like Facebook Insights.

  

All we need is a list of emails and/or phone numbers of your buyers.  Then we can upload this to Facebook and pull back very accurate demographic information on your buyers.  This is a HUGE asset! 

*Facebook needs a minimum of 1000 active emails and/or phone numbers to give us any data insights into your customers.

1. What industry(ies) does your target customer work for?
    
2. Who in the organization is responsible for making the purchase decision? 
    
3. What motivates your customers to buy your services?
    
4. How old are your buyers (typically, if applicable)?
