# Remodeler Marketing Playbook

Welcome to the Remodeler Marketing Playbook. This is your complete guide to building the most known and trusted remodeling brand in your market.

## Mission

Become the most known and trusted remodeler in your market.

## Core Frameworks

This playbook is built on two proven frameworks:

- **Marcus Sheridan's *Endless Customers*** - Revenue Team model, Big 5 content, Assignment Selling
- **Gary Vaynerchuk's *Day Trading Attention*** - Creative velocity, organic-first, platform fluency
