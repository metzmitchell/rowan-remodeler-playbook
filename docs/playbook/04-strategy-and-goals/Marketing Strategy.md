### **Value Proposition**

Define the type of value you create for your clients and customers.

* What is the [universal value proposition](https://kevin.me/universal/) for your business?  
  * The core unifying objective underpinning everything you do as a business.  
* What are the core outcomes people are hiring you for (jobs to be done)?  
  * I.e.:  
    * Risk Mitigation  
    * Revenue Growth  
    * More time  
    * Faster execution  
    * Lower cost/saving money  
    * Ego enhancement  
    * Confidence   
    * Ease of execution  
    * Experience  
  * Examples:  
    * Buying art  
      * NOT: Make the house look nicer  
      * YES: Enjoying your living space and showcasing your style to visitors  
* Going to the dentist  
  * NOT: Fixing a tooth  
  * YES: A healthy mouth and beautiful smile so I can be healthy and look my best  
* Buying an expensive camera  
  * NOT: Take beautiful photos  
  * YES: Document family memories in high resolution so I can look back in 20 years and remember things more clearly
