# **Strategy Call Questionnaires**

### **Consulting Call Questions**

Please answer the following questions to the best of your abilities. You can mark off "N/A" if a question doesn't apply to your situation, or leave blank if you do not know the answer. 

1. What is the primary challenge you are facing?   
2. What do you suspect are the main reasons for the current situation?   
3. What are you doing currently with your marketing?   
4. What's working well for you right now?   
5. What's not working well?  
6. Who are your ideal clients?   
7. Why should they choose you over anyone else?   
8. What is your current revenue?  
9. How many leads are you getting per month (broken out by service)?    
10. How many leads are you closing per month (close ratio or total number)?   
11. What is your cost per lead?   
12. What is your cost per new client/customer acquisition?  
13. Anything else you would like me to know about? 

### **Coaching Call Questions**

To help me prepare for our call, would you mind answering a couple questions below?

1. What is the purpose for our call?  
2. What are your top three questions?  
3. What do you think you should do?  
4. Where would you like to be 12 months from now?
