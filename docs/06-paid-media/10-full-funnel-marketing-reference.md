# Full Funnel Marketing Reference

Use this reference when planning advertising strategy to ensure coverage across all stages of the customer journey.

## Funnel Stages

- **Awareness** - Top of funnel: Introduce your brand to new audiences
- **Consideration** - Middle of funnel: Engage prospects evaluating options
- **Decision** - Bottom of funnel: Convert qualified leads to customers
- **Post-purchase** - Retention: Maintain relationships and encourage repeat business

Ensure your advertising mix addresses each stage appropriately for your business model and customer journey.
