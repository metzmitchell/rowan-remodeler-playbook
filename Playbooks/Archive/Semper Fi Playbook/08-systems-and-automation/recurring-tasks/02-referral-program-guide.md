# Referral Program Guide

Complete this checklist quarterly to remind existing clients about your referral program through various channels.

## Checklist

- [ ] **Send Email Reminder** - Send an email once a quarter to customers reminding them about the referral program

- [ ] **Check Physical Signage** - Ensure posters are posted in common areas reminding customers about the referral program

- [ ] **Include in Newsletter** - Ensure email newsletter always has mention of the customer referral program

## Best Practices

- Personalize email messages when possible
- Make referral process simple and clear
- Offer meaningful incentives
- Thank referrers promptly when referrals come in
