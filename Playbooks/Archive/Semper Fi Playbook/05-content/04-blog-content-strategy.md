**Owner:** SE (Content Creation), MM (Strategy)  
**Framework:** Marcus Sheridan Big 5 + SEO Best Practices

## Blog Strategy Overview

**Goal:** Become the #1 resource for remodeling questions in WNC and Greenville through comprehensive Big 5 content.

**Publishing frequency:** 1-2 posts/week (4-8/month)  
**Length:** 1,500-2,500 words (comprehensive, SEO-optimized)

## Content Calendar Framework

### Monthly Big 5 Distribution

| Week | Topic 1 | Topic 2 | Format |
|------|---------|---------|--------|

| **Week 2** | Comparisons | Reviews/Best | Blog post + video embed |

## Blog Post Structure (SEO + Conversion)

1. **Direct answer first 100 words** (AEO-optimized for AI search)
2. **Table of contents** (improves UX, helps search)
3. **Main content** with H2 headers, bullets, bold key points
4. **Real project example** with before/after images
5. **CTA** — link to calculator, quiz, or contact
6. **Internal links** to related Big 5 content

**See:** [AI in Marketing](../01-foundations/03-ai-in-marketing.md#workflow-1-blog-post-creation) for AI-assisted workflow

## SEO Optimization

**Keyword targeting:**
- Local: "kitchen remodel Asheville," "bathroom renovation Greenville SC"
- Long-tail: "how much does kitchen remodel cost in Asheville"
- Big 5 aligned: "kitchen remodel mistakes," "quartz vs granite countertops"

**Tools:**
- Google Search Console — keyword rankings
- AI for optimization — ChatGPT suggests keywords, meta descriptions

**See:** [SEO Fundamentals](../04-digital-presence/08-seo-fundamentals.md)

## Measuring Blog Performance

**Key metrics:**
- Pageviews (top 10 posts drive 80% of traffic)
- Time on page (target: 2+ min)
- Leads generated per post
- Assignment Selling usage (RJ tracks which posts he sends prospects)

**In Revenue Team meetings:** Which posts helped close deals?
