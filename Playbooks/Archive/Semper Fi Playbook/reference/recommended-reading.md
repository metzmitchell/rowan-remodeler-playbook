## Essential Books (In This Folder)

### Marcus Sheridan: *Endless Customers* (2025)

**Why read:** Foundation for Revenue Team model, Big 5 content framework, Assignment Selling

**Key concepts:**
- Revenue Team (marketing + sales unified)
- The Big 5 topics (pricing, problems, comparisons, reviews, what to expect)
- Assignment Selling (require content consumption before sales calls)
- The 4 Pillars (know buyer, be known, sell differently, be human)

**Where to find:**
- [Full book in docs folder](../endless-customers-full-book.md)
- [Summary with key takeaways](../../Marketing Thinking Jay/Marcus Sheridan.md)

**Most relevant for:** MM (strategy), RJ (Assignment Selling), SE (Big 5 content)

---

### Gary Vaynerchuk: *Day Trading Attention* (2024)

**Why read:** Framework for high-volume social content, organic-first paid strategy, platform fluency

**Key concepts:**
- Day Trading Attention (find underpriced attention, seize it)
- Creative velocity (volume matters)
- Organic first, amplify winners (test free, spend on proven)
- Platform-native content (adapt for each culture)
- Strategic Organic Content → Amplification → Post-Creative Strategy

**Where to find:**
- [Full book in docs folder](../Day_Trading_Attention_-_Gary_Vaynerchuk.md)
- [Summary with key quotes](../../Marketing Thinking Jay/Gary V.md)

**Most relevant for:** JH (creative production), SE (social posting), Performance Media Vendor (amplification)
